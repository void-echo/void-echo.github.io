Good Sentences From News

  • but the outrage, as it often does, is quickly turning into exaggerated interpretation.

  • commentary, especially in the United States, has zeroed in on what it means for “our” credibility.

  • And again the actions of Taliban foot soldiers undermined the leadership’s suggestions that, having taken power, they would moderate the brutality they have long been known for.

    塔利班领导人曾暗示，一旦掌权，他们将缓和长期以来他们为世人所知的残暴行为，但塔利班步兵的行动再次破坏了这一暗示。
