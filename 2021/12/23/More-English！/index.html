Exciting English For Writing. ♞

Mainly From CET-6 Vocabulary in Disorderly Order, Dolphin Books, 2020 ver.

PART 1

LIST 14.

WORDS

ENG ZH/JP
mutter that 抱怨
canterburry bell 风铃草
gamble 打赌
secular 世俗的
unprecedented ♞ 空前的
forge (空耳)[仿制], 打制, 锻造, 伪造
texture ♞ 质地
mingle 混合(with)
liberal 自由主义的
militant 激进分子(米利坦特)
dedicate to ♞ 献给, 用于
invariably 总是地, 不变地(显然也是个很容易活用的)

TIPS FOR WRITING

all in all >> In the aggregate(作为总体来讲)
— In the aggregate, women outlive men by 7 or more years.

friendly >> amiable (和蔼可亲的) >> accessible

eye healthy >> optic healthy 眼睛健康

after that >> thereafter
— Thereafter we had no further contact with them.

BEUTIFUL SENTENCES

The sun began to rise, the fog dissipated slowly.
太阳升起, 迷雾散去

Time can dilute everything.
时间会冲刷一切

Time can dilute everything, The wounds of the heart do not heal easily.
时间会冲刷一切, 但心灵之伤将持久存在. (与上面的句子正好相反, 正反都可以用.)

Corbett stared out the window and brooded.
首先, 没有 “of”. 其次, brood(凝望)很好用.

List 11

WORDS

ENG ZH
beware of /
amplify 放大
nitrogen 氮气
blunt ♞ 使迟钝，钝的，直言不讳的
tangle (with) 纠缠，乱作一团
allude (to) / (that) 暗示，暗指
analogy 比拟，类比
pastime 消遣，娱乐
intrigue v,n. 密谋，陷害，诡计

TIPS FOR WRITING

modify >> amend (修改，当然modify也不错)
We’ll have to amend our reports.

decline >> blunt(减少)
The bad weather had blunted our enthusiasm for camping.

晒黑 :: tan
Janny tanned quickly in the hot sun.

圆满谢幕 :: culminate
The conbention culminated in success.

BEUTIFUL SENTENCES

The voice of the teacher is crisp and assured.
老师的声音干净利落。

TIPS FOR WRITING

About ‘render’ :

He had to render an apology for his rudeness.
他不得不因他的粗鲁作出道歉
This renders it unnecessary for me to do anything.
这使我无需做任何事情。

certainly >> definitely
According to the data, We can definitely say that pollution is increasing.

keep doing >> peg away at doing
